For a valid offer there must be:

  1. Manifestation of intention to contract: Words or conduct that show intent to commit.
    1. Test: Would a reasonable person in the position of the offeree believe that his assent creates a contract.
      1. May look to the parties prior relationships or the custom in the industry.
    2. Advertisement: An advertisement is NOT an offer
      1. Exceptions
        1. Offer for reward
        2. Can be offer if specify as to quantity and expressly indicates who can accept.
  2. Certainity and Definitess of Terms: Offer is not required to contain all material terms however the terms must be certain and definite.
    1. Vague or ambiguous material terms: NOT an offer under either common law or UCC (Appropriate, fair and reasonable)
  3. Communication to an intended offeree:
